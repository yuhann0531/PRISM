Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account on the Public Road & Infrastructure System Monitor (PRISM), you agree to abide by these Terms of Service. PRISM is a dedicated transparency platform operated by the City Government of San Juan.

2. Purpose of the Platform

PRISM is designed exclusively for reporting and tracking public infrastructure issues within San Juan City. Any misuse of the platform will result in immediate account suspension.

3. Data Privacy Act of 2012

In compliance with Republic Act No. 10173, the City Government of San Juan collects your personal information solely for the purpose of verifying your identity and sending updates regarding your reports.

4. Accuracy of Information

Users are expected to provide truthful, accurate, and complete information when submitting a report. Uploaded photos must clearly show the reported issue.
