About PRISM

PRISM is a public infrastructure monitoring system that helps citizens report issues, contractors update project progress, and city admins manage reports and public works in one place.

Report an Issue Go to Dashboard

Mission

Make infrastructure updates clearer, faster, and easier for the community to follow.

Vision

A city where public works are transparent and citizens can participate with confidence.

Purpose

Connect citizens, contractors, and administrators through organized reports and project tracking.

What PRISM Can Do

  • Submit infrastructure reports with photos.
  • Track project status and completion.
  • Manage contractors, users, and citizen reports.
  • Publish notices about road work and repairs.
